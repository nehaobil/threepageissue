How can you participate in fighting ocean acidification?

Reduce your carbon footprint!

The biggest way to participate in mitigating ocean acidification is to reduce your carbon footprint. This can be done in multiple ways such as using less water, recycling plastic waste, shopping sustainably, etc. Living a sustainable lifestyle and reducing your carbon footprint will effectively improve many environmental issues, including ocean acidification.

Support environmentally-focused organizations!

Donate or give support to organizations that work towards protecting the oceans. Even if you cannot support financially, there are a multitude of opportunities to support by participating in events such as local beach clean ups or signing petitions! Some organizations to choose from are: The Ocean Project, Oceana, Australian Institute of Marine Sciences.
